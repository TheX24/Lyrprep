Welcome to Lyrprep!

Here's how to get started:

  1. Paste your lyrics or use the search button to find lyrics
  2. Customize the processing options in Settings
  3. Copy the formatted lyrics with one click
  4. Open AMLL TTML Tool
  5. Make sure that "Word separator" is set to "\" (backslash) and "Enable special prefixes" is checked
  6. Paste the lyrics and click on import

All processing happens in your browser - your data stays private!
